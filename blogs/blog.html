Princeton Energy Consumption

by Jiahuan Kang

Posted on August 28, 2013 at 10:00 PM

Since 2009, Princeton used 6,596,437 MWH of energy, which is equivalent to 4,619,354 tons of CO2. This energy consumption is heavily affacted by the tempareture and length of the day. People consumes more energy and produce more CO2 at lower temperature and on longer days. Better insulation, installing smart-switch, using solar power are great ways to reduce energy consumption and thus environmental impact.
